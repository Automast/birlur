Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2024

Birlur ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you join our waitlist through our landing page.

1. Information We Collect

When you sign up for our waitlist, we collect the following personal information:

  • Name: To personalize our communications with you.
  • Email Address: To send you updates, news, and notifications regarding our launch and services.
  • Website Type: To understand our audience's needs and tailor our product development.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To manage our waitlist and provide you with information about your spot.
  • To send you marketing communications, such as early access invitations, product updates, and special offers.
  • To conduct internal research and analysis to improve our product.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. Your form submission data is processed by Formspree, our form backend provider. Their use of your information is governed by their own privacy policy. We will not share your information with any other third parties without your explicit consent, unless required by law.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no internet-based site can be 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to opt-out of our communications at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our emails. You can also contact us to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
